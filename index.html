Welcome to PIG game! Nope, I know what you are thinking, but sadly, there are no pigs involved in this game.
🎲 Game Rules 🎲
🐷 Pig Mode 🐷
- Each player will take turn to roll the dice, if a 1 is rolled, their current score and turns are forfeited!
- Total score is safe in pig mode, unless it is reduced by gamble event.
🐷🐷 Two Dice Pig Mode 🐷🐷
- Two dices are used in two dice pig mode, if either a dice shows a 1, their current score and turns are forfeited!
- If both dices shows a 1, the player's TOTAL score is resetted!
⚡ Gamble Event ⚡
- Each player is entitled to only one gamble event! Where they can choose to gamble or decline it. Decide wisely!
- Gamble event will trigger when player reaches half the winning points!
