  • Each player will take turn to roll the dice, if a 1 is rolled, their current score and turns are forefited!
  • In pig mode, only one dice is used.
  • In two dice pig mode, two dices are used. If any of the dice is rolled as a 1, the player loses their current score and have their turn forefited.
  • Current score will be passed into total score if a player choses to pass their turn
  • Total score is safe, except in TWO DICE PIG mode, if double 1's are rolled, the total score is resetted to 0!
  • First player to reach the desired point wins(default is 100)!
